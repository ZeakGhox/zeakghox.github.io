Predicting Reversal of High Blood Pressure Using Machine Learning

MSDS 696 – Data Science Practicum II

Week 2 – Progress Report


Project Details

When a person is diagnosed with high blood pressure, medication is frequently used as both the initial and long-term treatment option. Anecdotal reporting exists of individuals who were able to reverse their high blood pressure diagnosis and eliminate the need for medication through a combination of diet and/or exercise. This project seeks to identify, with machine learning, methods that have a demonstrated ability to reverse a high blood pressure diagnosis.
